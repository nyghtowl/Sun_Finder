About Sun Finder

A commone question in San Francisco is, "What's the weather where I'm going?". There is such a micro-climate that the weather varies drastically just between neighborhoods.

I was trying to pick a final Hackbright final project in late April 2013 when I went for a hike. My Apple weather app said cloudy only even though it was raining. I wanted a simple app to quickly tell me where to find sun in the Bay Area for the hike. Thus, the idea for Sun Finder.

The web application's functional goal is to provide a quick search option based on micro-climate location. The underlying goal is to give me something to build my coding skills. If you like this app and want to see it improve, please consider giving through and send feedback.

Additional acknowledgements and resources utilized in the app development:
