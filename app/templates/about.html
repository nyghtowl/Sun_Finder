About Sun Finder

In April 2013, I was trying to go for a hike and I was faced with a common question since I moved to San Francisco over a decade ago: "What's the weather where I'm going?". Its a common challenge when the weather varies drastically just between neighborhoods.

That day of the hike, the weather report for SF was just cloudy even though it was raining where I was.I wanted an app to quickly tell me where to find sun in the Bay Area for the hike. Thus, the idea for Sun Finder.

The goal is to provide a quick search option based on the Bay Area neighborhood / area to get weather info. Its a work in progress to expand features and functionality but the goal will stay the same to provide a fast and simple answer to what should be a simple question.

Additional acknowledgements and resources utilized in the app development:
