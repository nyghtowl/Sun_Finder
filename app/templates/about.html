About Sun Finder

A commone question in San Francisco is, "What's the weather where I'm going?". There is such a micro-climate that the weather varies drastically just between neighborhoods.

While picking my final Hackbright project in late April 2013, I went for a hike. My Apple weather app said cloudy with no indication of rain even though it was raining. The app reported a much higher level weather results for SF and I needed something to quickly tell me where to find sun (or at least no rain) in the Bay Area. Thus, the idea for Sun Finder.

The web application's functional goal is to provide a quick search option based on micro-climate location. The underlying goal is to give me something to build my coding skills. If you like this app and want to see it improve, please consider donating through and/or send feedback.
